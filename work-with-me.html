Work With Me

Clear thinking for science, strategy, and communication.

I help people turn complex ideas into structured decisions, stronger presentations, and more focused next steps. My work is especially shaped by computational astrobiology, research workflows, science communication, and early-stage building.

Decision Brief

For people or teams who need complex information turned into a clear, structured, decision-ready document.

Best for

Research strategy, technical tradeoffs, literature synthesis, early-stage project direction, or complex decisions that need clearer framing.

You receive

  • 1–3 page written brief
  • core question, context, and assumptions
  • comparison of possible paths
  • risks, tradeoffs, and recommended next steps

Science Communication & Presentation Support

For researchers, students, educators, or teams who need help making complex ideas clearer, more visual, and easier to present.

Best for

Conference talks, research posters, public-facing presentations, educational content, grant/project narratives, or complex ideas that need a stronger story.

You receive

  • presentation storyline and slide flow
  • sharper framing for a research talk, poster, or public-facing presentation
  • audience-specific messaging and simplified explanations
  • speaker notes, talking points, or feedback on visual logic

Clarity Session

For students, early-career researchers, creators, or builders who need help thinking through a complex next step.

Best for

Research direction, career questions, project planning, communication strategy, or organizing scattered ideas into a clearer path.

You receive

  • focused 45–60 minute conversation
  • identification of priorities, blockers, and next steps
  • short follow-up summary with key takeaways
  • optional lightweight resources or reflection prompts

Community, Mentorship, and Project-Based Work

I try to keep some of my work freely available, especially for students, early-career researchers, and curious people who are still finding their path. At the same time, my time and energy are limited and valuable, and they are currently spread across multiple PhD projects, research collaborations, science communication, mentoring, and personal creative work.

For that reason, I think of my work in three lanes:

  • Free resources: public writing, videos, templates, and occasional open Q&A.
  • Student and early-career support: short conversations, mentorship, or lightweight guidance when capacity allows.
  • Project-based work: deeper, time-intensive support such as decision briefs, presentation support, research communication, or structured project guidance.

If you are unsure where your request fits, reach out with a short note about what you’re working on and what kind of support would be most helpful.
